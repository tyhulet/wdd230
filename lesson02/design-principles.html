Hick's Law

Google

Google.com

Hick's law is a desigin principle that talks about how a person will take longer to make a descion when prestented with more options. in simple terms if a person has more options there decsion time will be longer. The google website is a perfect example of this because when a person goes to this site, they are given a single option which would decrease there decsion makeing time.
